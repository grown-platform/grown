Liar's Dice

Liar's Dice is online multiplayer only for 2–6 players. Create a game and share the link, or join one — then use the waiting room to ready up.

Each player has 5 dice. Bid how many of a face are on the table (across all hands). Raise the bid or call "Liar!". Wrong caller — or caught bluffer — loses a die. Lose all 5 and you're out. Last player standing wins. (Each die counts as its own face — no wilds.)
