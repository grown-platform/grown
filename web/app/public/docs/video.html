Video

A YouTube-style video library for your workspace — upload, browse and watch videos hosted on infrastructure you control.

Open Video → Browse all apps

Overview

Video gives your organisation its own video product. Upload a file and it appears as a thumbnail tile with a title, description and duration; click to watch in a full player that remembers where you left off. Group videos into playlists, add subtitles, and share a clip with specific teammates or with anyone via a public watch link — all without handing your footage to a third-party platform.

Features

  • Library. Every video shows a thumbnail, title, description and duration; a watched badge and resume bar reflect your progress.
  • Watch page. A full player with a YouTube-style watch view; playback position is saved as you go so you can pick up where you left off on any device.
  • Playlists. Organise videos into named playlists and play through them in order.
  • Captions & subtitles. Attach WebVTT caption tracks in multiple languages with selectable labels.
  • Share with people. Search your organisation's directory and share a video with specific members.
  • Public watch links. Mint a tokenised public link (optionally with an expiry) that lets anyone watch without an account — revoke it any time.
  • Upload & manage. Upload with a progress bar, edit title/description/thumbnail, download the original, or delete videos you no longer need.
  • Range streaming. Videos stream over range-capable HTTP, so seeking is instant and large files don't need to fully buffer.

How it works

Video stores uploaded files in the workspace's object storage and serves them over range-capable HTTP under the workspace origin. Per-user watch progress, playlists, captions and shares live in the platform database. Public links resolve through an unauthenticated token endpoint, so a shared clip plays for viewers who aren't signed in.

Storage
Video files in workspace object storage; metadata in the platform database
Streaming
Range-capable HTTP (seek & resume)
Sharing
Per-user shares + tokenised public watch links
Auth
Workspace single sign-on (OIDC); public links need no account
