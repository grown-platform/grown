Music

Stream your own music collection — upload your tracks, organise them into playlists, and play them anywhere with a full-featured web player.

Open Music → Browse all apps

Overview

Music is your personal streaming service, hosted on your own workspace. Upload audio files and they land in a browsable library with title, artist, album and artwork. Build playlists, mark the songs you love, and stream anything on demand — the audio is served straight from your workspace's object storage with range requests, so seeking and resuming are instant.

Features

  • Your library. Every uploaded track shows its title, artist, album, duration and artwork; search across all three fields to find anything fast.
  • Playlists. Create and name playlists, add or remove tracks, and drag to reorder — the order is saved server-side.
  • Liked songs. Heart any track to collect it in a dedicated Liked view you can play in one tap.
  • Full player. A persistent player bar with play/pause, previous/next, a seek scrubber, volume, shuffle, and repeat (off / all / one).
  • Queue. Play a whole list or add tracks to the up-next queue; the player walks the queue honouring shuffle and repeat.
  • Upload with metadata. Drop in an audio file — duration is probed in the browser before upload, and you can set title, artist, album and artwork. A progress bar tracks the transfer.
  • Edit & manage. Rename track metadata, swap artwork, download the original file, or delete tracks you no longer want.
  • Range streaming. Tracks stream over range-capable HTTP, so jumping around a song or resuming playback is immediate.

How it works

Music stores your audio in the workspace's object storage and keeps track and playlist metadata in the platform database. The web player streams files directly over range-capable HTTP under the workspace origin, so playback, seeking and resume all work without a separate media server.

Storage
Audio files in workspace object storage; metadata in the platform database
Streaming
Range-capable HTTP (seek & resume)
Auth
Workspace single sign-on (OIDC)
